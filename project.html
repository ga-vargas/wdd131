Welcome to Argentina

Argentina Facts

  • Capital: Buenos Aires
  • Population: ~45 million
  • Language: Spanish
  • Currency: Argentine Peso (ARS)
  • Area: 2.78 million km²

Argentina is a land of vast landscapes, diverse culture, and unique attractions. From the majestic peaks of the Andes Mountains to the vibrant streets of Buenos Aires, this country offers a wide variety of experiences. Whether you're interested in outdoor adventures, cultural explorations, or simply soaking in beautiful scenery, Argentina has something for everyone. Discover the warmth and hospitality of its people, the unique traditions and customs, and the awe-inspiring natural wonders that make Argentina a must-visit destination in South America.

Why You Should Visit Argentina:

Relevant fact #1: Explore the vibrant culture and iconic landmarks of Buenos Aires, Argentina's bustling capital.

Relevant fact #2: Discover Ushuaia, the southernmost city in the world, located in Tierra del Fuego.

Relevant fact #3: Experience the Andes and Mount Aconcagua, the highest peak in the Americas.
